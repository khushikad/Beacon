BEACON PARK

Beacon Park is ranked #982 in California Elementary Schools and ranked #136 in California Middle Schools. Schools are ranked on their performance on state-required tests, graduation, and how well they prepare their students for high school.

ABOUT US

Beacon Park is a TK-8 Grade school in the Great Park community with a diverse student body and supportive parent community. Our highly qualified teachers challenge students to deeper levels of critical thinking and understanding with their grade level content while using multiple instructional strategies to engage and meet the needs of all learners. We believe that everyone should be encouraged and supported to take risks and do their personal best.

OUR TEACHER SERVICES

In our classrooms, our staff implements a rigorous and innovative instructional program. We leverage technology and focus on creative design and project-based assignments for students. Student learning is reflected in presentations of their work, persuasive essays, and written journals, as well as multimedia archives. Our Middle School students enjoy a rich menu of engaging electives that complement their core academic program.

SCIENCE PROGRAMS

The S.T.E.M. department hosts three major events throughout the school year. The purpose of these events is to further peak student interest in STEM related fields.
